Deron Clothing - Terms and Conditions

1. Return Policy

All goods sold by Deron Clothing are of Grade One quality. Customers may return items within one week (7 days) of purchase. Returns will only be accepted if the items are in their original condition, unworn, and with all original tags attached. Proof of purchase must be provided for all returns. Refunds will be issued to the original form of payment once the returned items are received and inspected. Shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer, unless the return is due to a defect or error on the part of Deron Clothing.

2. Exchanges

Exchanges are subject to the availability of the desired item. If the desired item is unavailable, a refund will be processed.

3. Defective or Incorrect Items

In the event that you receive a defective or incorrect item, please contact our customer service within 48 hours of receiving the goods. Deron Clothing will cover the return shipping costs for defective or incorrect items.

4. Non-returnable Items

Items purchased on sale or clearance are not eligible for return or exchange. Underwear and swimwear are not eligible for return due to hygiene reasons.
